# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `quant-qmt` to be a respectful, practical, and collaborative open-source project.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful in language and tone
- assume good intent
- focus on the problem, not the person
- share actionable feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or insults
- hostile personal attacks
- deliberate intimidation
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that do not fit this standard.
